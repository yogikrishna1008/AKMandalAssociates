The Advocate
Justice is not served by speed alone — it is won by preparation, precedent, and principle.

Advocate Ashish Kumar Mandal has spent over two decades building one of Rourkela's most respected civil and criminal practices. Enrolled with the Bar Council of Odisha in 2004, he holds dual institutional mandates that speak directly to his standing: empanelled as legal counsel for Canara Bank for debt recovery and financial litigation, and retained as standing counsel by United India Insurance Company for MACT tribunal matters.

His practice extends across four states — Odisha, Jharkhand, West Bengal, and Assam — with active matters before the ACJM Court, District & Sessions Courts, the High Court of Orissa, and the Calcutta High Court. This breadth is not incidental; it reflects a disciplined approach to complex multi-jurisdictional mandates that few single-practitioner advocates in Rourkela can match.
